Careers Lawyers Trainees Business Support Newsletter Contact us Bernardo Maria Lobo Xavier Partner Areas & Industries Related Content Contact Bernardo Lobo Xavier joined the firm in 2006 and became a partner in 2008. He is head of a corporate team. Bernardo has represented national clients in both M&A and real estate transactions. He also provides advice and regular assistance on different aspects of the ongoing commercial activities of our clients. From 1990 to 2005 he practised law at Os&oacute;rio de Castro, Verde Pinho, Vieira Peres, Lobo Xavier e Associados &ndash; Sociedade de Advogados. He became a partner at this firm in 2002. "assertiveness, knowledge and experience." Chambers Europe Education Law Degree (Coimbra Faculty of Law, 1989). Languages Portuguese, English and Spanish. Memberships Portuguese Bar Association (admitted in 1992).
