Careers Lawyers Trainees Business Support Newsletter Contact us Catarina Almeida Andrade Principal Associate Areas & Industries Contact Catarina Almeida Andrade joined the firm in November 2021. She is a member of the corporate department, as well as a member of Team Genesis . As a tax specialist with a particular focus on tax structuring, private wealth and corporate legal advisory, Catarina has been helping national and international companies evolve throughout their full lifecycle and has developed a strong understanding of the particular needs associated with fundraising, growth/scaling and exit. Catarina&rsquo;s professional life started out in Big 4 consulting (KPMG and, subsequently, Deloitte), where she focused on advising clients on private wealth management, international tax matters, and financing operations (i.e. mezzanine loans, derivatives, options, swaps, futures) for medium and large businesses, while also helping navigate high net-worth individuals through the intricate world of taxation. After leaving the consulting world, Catarina joined RFF & Associados, where she continued to provide tax and legal support to private and corporate investors.
