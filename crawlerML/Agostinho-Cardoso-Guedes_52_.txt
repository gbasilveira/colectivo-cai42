Careers Lawyers Trainees Business Support Newsletter Contact us Agostinho Cardoso Guedes Consultant Areas & Industries Related Content Contact Agostinho Cardoso Guedes joined the firm in 2006. He is a member of the corporate and M&A team. Agostinho has been involved in the negotiation of agreements relating to industrial equipment procurement, civil construction and financing, particularly in the areas of energy and telecommunications. Professor at the Cat&oacute;lica Porto School of Law. He was also a Lecturer at the Oporto Entrepreneur Institute (Instituto Empresarial Portuense (AEP) from 1993 to 2000 and at the Oporto Management School (Escola de Gest&atilde;o do Porto) from 2000 to 2002. Member of the Oporto Commercial Arbitration Center (Centro de Arbitragem Comercial da C&acirc;mara de Com&eacute;rcio e Ind&uacute;stria do Porto) and member of the General Committee of the Portuguese Judicial College (Conselho Geral do Centro de Estudos Judici&aacute;rios). Former Dean of Cat&oacute;lica Porto Law School from 2006 to 2013, and former president of the National Evaluation Committee of the Portuguese Bar Association (Comiss&atilde;o Nacional de Avalia&ccedil;&atilde;o da Ordem dos Advogados, 2006). Previously, Agostinho practised law at Os&oacute;rio de Castro, Verde Pinho, Vieira Peres, Lobo Xavier e Associados &ndash; Sociedade de Advogados from 1989 to 2005. Author and co-author of several books and articles, in Portuguese and foreign reviews. Education Law Degree (Cat&oacute;lica Porto School of Law, 1985). Master's Degree in Civil Legal Sciences (Coimbra Faculty of Law, 1998). Ph.D in Civil Legal Sciences (Cat&oacute;lica Porto School of Law, 2005). Languages Portuguese, English, French, Italian and Spanish. Memberships Portuguese Bar Association (1992). Portuguese Arbitration Association.
