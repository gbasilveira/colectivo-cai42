Careers Lawyers Trainees Business Support Newsletter Contact us Bernardo Almeida Azevedo Of Counsel Areas & Industries Related Content Contact Bernardo Almeida Azevedo joined the firm in 2008. He is a member of the administrative and public law team. In particular, Bernardo works essentially as a consultant in the preparation of studies in the areas of the general administrative law and public procurement. He also regularly advises on those areas both private and public sector clients from the education, health and community funds sectors. Bernardo is an Assistant Professor at Coimbra Faculty of Law with a PhD in Legal and Political Sciences. From 2008 to 2013 he was a member of the Superior Counsel of the Administrative and Tax Courts. Education Law Degree ( Coimbra Faculty of Law , 1992). LL.M. in Administrative Law ( Coimbra Faculty of Law , 2002). PhD in Legal and Political Sciences ( Coimbra Faculty of Law , 2018). Languages Portuguese, English and French. Memberships Portuguese Bar Association (admitted in 1994).
