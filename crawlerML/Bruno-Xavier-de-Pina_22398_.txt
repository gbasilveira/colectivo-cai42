Careers Lawyers Trainees Business Support Newsletter Contact us Bruno Xavier de Pina International Consultant Areas & Industries Related Content Contact Bruno Xavier de Pina joined the firm in 2021. Bruno is a profound expert on the Lusophone world, in particular on the Angolan and Cape Verdean markets, with relevant experience in cross-border matters. He has been dedicated to the sectors of telecommunications, electronics and aviation, oil and gas and manufacturing. Bruno Xavier de Pina was a partner and coordinator of the Angola International Group (Angola Desk and BCSA, an Angolan law firm) at PLMJ, having also worked at Raposo Bernardo & Associados, Miranda & Associados and Deloitte as a tax consultant. He is a lawyer since 2004, being specialised in advising on foreign investment operations and on projects investment&rsquo;s reorganization. Bruno Xavier de Pina has been distinguished by international directories such as Chambers and IFLR. In 2017, he was recognized with the Iberian Lawyer's "Forty under 40" award and with the Outstanding Achievement award, received on that same occasion. &ldquo;Bruno Xavier de Pina is an excellent lawyer.
