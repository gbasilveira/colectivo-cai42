is an international consultant based in Brussels since 2021. She is a member of the European and Competition law team. From 2015 to 2021, she worked as an academic legal researcher while based in Kuwait, South Africa and the UAE. She was a lecturer of Public Law at Cat&oacute;lica Porto School of Law. Previously, she worked with the firm between 2008 and 2011. Catarina has published several articles in national and international law reviews. Education Law Degree (Cat&oacute;lica Porto School of Law, 2007). LL.M. in European Law (College of Europe Bruges, Belgium, 2008). PhD in Competition law (Cat&oacute;lica Porto School of Law, 2021). Languages Portuguese, English, French and Spanish. Memberships Portuguese Bar Association (admitted in 2012).
